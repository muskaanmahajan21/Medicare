Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online via our website, call us at 9656372834, or visit our clinic directly.

Do you offer online consultations?

No! We offer a contact option to book appointments and a chatbot for quick consultation discussions.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers. Please call our front desk to confirm your plan coverage.

Can I access my medical records online?

No, not possible now , our developer team works on that in future.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID proof, insurance card (if applicable), any past medical records, and a list of medications.

Do you provide emergency services?

We handle minor emergencies. For life-threatening emergencies, please dial 108 or visit the nearest hospital immediately.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

Do you offer vaccination services?

Yes, we offer flu shots, COVID-19 vaccines, and routine immunizations for children and adults.

Can I consult a doctor virtually?

No! you have to visit to the hospital.

When will I receive my lab test results?

Lab results are usually available within 24 to 48 hours. You can collect from the hospital.

What safety measures are you taking against COVID-19?

We follow all WHO guidelines, including mandatory masks, frequent sanitization, and temperature checks for all visitors.

Can I get a second opinion at your hospital?

Yes, we encourage second opinions. Our experienced specialists will review your reports and provide detailed advice.
